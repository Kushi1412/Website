## Services (4 pillars)

1. **AI-Enabled Finance Operations** — Automating repetitive, high-volume finance processes (reconciliations, entity reporting, compliance checks). Start boring, scale smart.
2. **Finance Transformation Design** — Cross-functional operating model design. Not "Finance-led with stakeholder input" — truly cross-functional with shared ownership and governance.
3. **Post-Merger Finance Integration** — Due diligence + integration of acquired company's financial setup: entity structure, governance, processes, compliance. Separating integration (urgent, compliance-driven) from transformation (strategic, mandate-driven).
4. **Entity Governance & Structuring** — Entity setup, PE lifecycle, restructuring, wind-down, exit-readiness across jurisdictions.

**Upcoming addition:** Valuation services — business case valuations and estimated integration/transformation costs. (Still to be scoped.)
