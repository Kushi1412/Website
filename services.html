Turning complexity into clarity

Every engagement is tailored. These are the areas where I work — typically with CFOs, Group Finance leaders, and transformation programme teams navigating change at scale.

01

AI-Enabled Finance Operations

Most finance teams know AI matters but struggle to move beyond pilots and proof-of-concepts. I help organisations identify the right use cases, build business cases, and design implementation roadmaps that connect AI capabilities to real operational outcomes.

This isn't about chasing technology — it's about understanding which finance processes are ripe for intelligent automation and building the governance to deploy it responsibly.

Typical deliverables

  • AI opportunity assessment across finance functions
  • Use case prioritisation and business case development
  • Implementation roadmap with governance framework
  • Vendor evaluation and selection support
  • Change management and adoption planning
02

Finance Transformation Design

Finance transformation programmes often stall because they lack a clear operating model to build towards. I design target operating models grounded in how the organisation actually works — not theoretical frameworks that collapse on contact with reality.

This includes process taxonomy design, RACI mapping across functions, and governance structures that scale with organisational complexity. Particularly relevant for multinationals managing entity lifecycles across jurisdictions.

Typical deliverables

  • Current-state assessment and gap analysis
  • Target operating model design (L1–L4 process taxonomy)
  • Cross-functional governance framework
  • Transformation roadmap and workstream definition
  • Stakeholder alignment and leadership communication
03

Post-Merger Finance Integration

M&A transactions create enormous pressure on finance teams. The work between signing and operational integration — harmonising chart of accounts, aligning reporting standards, standing up new entities, consolidating processes — is where deals succeed or fail operationally.

I lead or support finance integration workstreams, bringing structure and pace to the work that typically falls between corporate development and BAU finance.

Typical deliverables

  • Day 1 readiness planning for finance
  • Chart of accounts harmonisation
  • Reporting and consolidation alignment
  • Entity setup and cross-border structuring support
  • Integration programme governance and tracking
