Our Approach

We start in finance. We don't stop there.

Finance transformation fails when it's scoped as a pure finance initiative. Every operating model change requires people outside finance to change how they work. Every post-merger integration needs cross-functional alignment that doesn't exist on day one.

We work across finance, legal, tax, HR and technology — because that's how reality works. Our IMO experience has taught us that cross-functional ownership is what determines whether a transformation lands or stalls.

Whether we're brought in as strategic advisors or embedded hands-on in the team, we build solutions that work across the organisation — and that last beyond our engagement.

01

Assess

Understanding the current setup, pain points and transformation ambitions through structured discovery and process mapping.

02

Design

Building the target operating model with clear workstreams, milestones and cross-functional ownership — grounded in what's achievable.

03

Implement

Working alongside the team to execute changes, build governance structures and ensure the transformation sticks.

04

Handover

Embedding knowledge and transferring ownership so the organisation can sustain and evolve the model independently.
